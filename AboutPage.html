ABOUT



In 2019, I received the Rising Star Award at the Video Innovators Conference, and in 2020, I was honored with the Visual Excellence Award at the Digital Creators Festival. My work has appeared in various publications, and one of my proudest moments was winning Best Photography at the Philippine Arts Festival. I also had my first solo exhibit at a major art gallery, marking my growing influence in the visual arts community.

I'm John Mazel, a photographer and creative director. My passion lies in capturing moments that tell a story, whether through still photography or motion pictures. I look forward to sharing my journey and work with you.
